# aiMate Logo Generation Prompts

Based on the aiMate project philosophy of providing free, safe, and sovereign AI for all Kiwis with a focus on privacy, workspace collaboration, and cultural awareness.

---

## Prompt 1: Kiwi Identity & Authenticity

**Style:** Modern, friendly, approachable
**Concept:** New Zealand-focused AI companion

```
A modern, minimalist logo featuring a stylized kiwi bird (New Zealand's national symbol) merged with abstract AI neural network nodes. The kiwi is formed by connected dots and lines suggesting both the bird's organic shape and digital connectivity. Color palette: Deep purple (#764ba2) and vibrant teal, with clean white space. The design should feel warm and approachable rather than corporate or cold. Include subtle fern frond patterns in the negative space. Style: flat design, geometric, friendly, tech-forward but grounded in nature.
```

---

## Prompt 2: Privacy & Data Sovereignty

**Style:** Secure, trustworthy, shield-like
**Concept:** Privacy-first workspace guardian

```
A logo combining a protective shield with an open hand, symbolizing both security and openness. Inside the shield, subtle binary code or data streams flow upward like smoke or mist, representing data that stays under user control. The shield has soft, rounded edges to feel approachable rather than militaristic. Color gradient from deep purple (#667eea) to violet (#764ba2). Include a small silver fern detail at the base. The overall feel should be "guardian" not "fortress" - protective but welcoming. Style: clean, modern, slightly abstract, professional yet warm.
```

---

## Prompt 3: Workspace & Collaboration Hub

**Style:** Connected, collaborative, organized
**Concept:** AI workspace platform for teams

```
An interconnected workspace visualization: multiple chat bubbles or workspace windows arranged in a circular or orbital pattern around a central AI core (represented as a glowing purple sphere or node). Thin lines connect the workspaces, suggesting collaboration and knowledge sharing. Each workspace bubble contains subtle icons: code brackets, document lines, lightbulb for ideas. The design should convey "organized creativity" and "connected thinking spaces." Purple gradient palette (#667eea to #764ba2) with white workspace bubbles. Style: isometric or 2.5D, clean lines, modern SaaS aesthetic, friendly and productive.
```

## General Design Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Purple gradient (#667eea to #764ba2)
- Accent: Teal, silver fern green
- Background: White or very light purple

**Typography Suggestions (if including text):**
- Sans-serif, modern but friendly
- Lowercase "aiMate" or "ai mate" for approachability
- Avoid overly technical/corporate fonts

**Cultural Considerations:**
- Incorporate MƒÅori design elements respectfully (koru, fern, traditional patterns)

**Overall Tone:**
- Friendly, not corporate
- Secure, not fortress-like
- Modern, not cold
- Accessible, not dumbed-down
- Professional workspace tool, not consumer chat toy

## Usage Notes

These prompts can be used with:
- DALL-E 3
- Midjourney
- Stable Diffusion
- Professional designers as creative briefs

**Recommended Approach:**
1. Generate variations using prompts 1-4
2. Combine elements from favorites
3. Refine with specific feedback
4. Test at different sizes (favicon, header, social media)
5. Ensure it works in monochrome for versatility

---

## Design Iterations

After initial generation, consider variations that:
- Work well at 16x16px (favicon)
- Scale to large format (website headers)
- Look good in monochrome/single color
- Are recognizable without text
- Reflect the specific personality mode (Kiwi Mate, Mental Health Guardian, etc.)
