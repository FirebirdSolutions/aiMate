## Tracking Tips

### URL Parameters

Add tracking parameters to identify which group respondents came from:

```
https://your-domain.com/?source=facebook-seattle-tech
https://your-domain.com/?source=facebook-pdx-startups
https://your-domain.com/?source=reddit-machinelearning
https://your-domain.com/?ref=twitter
```

### Naming Convention

Suggested format: `source={platform}-{community-name}`

Examples:
- `facebook-seattle-tech-group`
- `facebook-ai-enthusiasts-pdx`
- `reddit-localllama`
- `twitter-post-jan2025`
- `linkedin-network`

This helps you:
- Track which communities are most engaged
- Identify best-performing channels
- Tailor follow-up messaging
- Select diverse alpha testers

## Posting Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm or 7pm-9pm local time
- **Avoid**: Early mornings, late nights, weekends (varies by group)

### Engagement
- Reply to comments quickly
- Thank people who complete it
- Share insights ("Already got 50+ responses - thank you!")
- Update with milestones ("100 responses! Survey closes Friday")

### Group Rules
- **Always check posting rules first!**
- Ask moderators for permission if needed
- Some groups have "Self-Promotion Saturday" or specific days
- Don't spam - post once per group

### Follow-Up
- After 48 hours: "Thanks to everyone who filled this out! Still open for another day if you haven't yet"
- After closing: "Survey closed! Thank you to all 200+ respondents. Alpha invites going out next week!"
